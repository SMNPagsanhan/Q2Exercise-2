Science Topics

Momentum

  • Momentum describes an object’s “quantity of motion."
  • It is a vector quantity. Its direction is the same as the velocity.
  • The SI unit of momentum is kg m/s.
  • The product of the mass of an object and its velocity.
  • Momentum is directly proportional to velocity.
  • For objects with the same mass but different velocities, the object with the higher velocity will have a greater momentum.
